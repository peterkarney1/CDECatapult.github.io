Who, what, how?

Who?

The Digital Catapult Open Health Data Platform has been developed by the Connected Digital Economy Catapult and Mastodon C to stimulate data science innovation in the UK.

What?

We want to show how you can use linked open health data in new and exciting ways - with worked examples and demonstrations of what is possible - as well as giving you easy access to the raw data.

How?

Dive into the How To's section to see some worked examples. Have a look at the resources on the site for an idea of what you can do to get started, or join us on Google+ to meet other innovators working with this data.
